About Us · Meet Bebo

Our Story

Bebo is the adorable Shih Tzu who inspired everything about The Fluffy Co. Her gentle nature, irresistible charm, and absolute love for being pampered is the heartbeat behind this studio.

Founded by Kavita Banddekar, The Fluffy Co was born from a simple belief — that every pet deserves the same love, patience, and expert care that Bebo receives every single day.

From professional grooming to the deeply unique service of Animal Communication, we treat every pet like family — because to us, they truly are.
